Notes and Limitations

  1. The counter won't be accurate if a sentence contains acronyms or names with periods. For instance, "J. D. Johnson" will count as three sentences. "J.D. Johnson" will count as two.
  2. A paragraph is a block of text. Paragraphs are separated by line breaks.
  3. The character count includes spaces.
